Welcome to Scary Meter!


Have you ever wondered just how scary a movie is? If so, you've come to the right place! Movies here are ranked based on how Scary, Creepy, Gory and Jumpy they are instead of on quality. These scores are compiled from user ratings. Please feel free to vote in as many categories for as many movies as you'd like. For more information, you may reference our About Us page.
